# My Current Workout Routine

## Push/Pull/Legs Split

The goal here is to train 5 days a week, hitting pull days and push days twice, and legs once. 

## Push Day
  - Flat Bench 5x5
  - Incline Db Bench 3 x 10
   - Cable Flys 3x10
  - Tricep pulldowns 3x10
  - Rope pulldowns 3x10

## Leg Day
- Squats 5x5
- Hack Squats 5x5
- Leg Extentions 3x10
- Leg Curls 3x10
- Calve Raises 3x10
- Hip Abductors 3x10

## Pull Day
- Pullups to failure
- Bent over rows 3x8
- Cable Rows 3x10
- Striaght Arm Pulldowns 3x10
- Hammer Curls 3x10
- Regular Curls 3x10
- Drag Curls 3x10
